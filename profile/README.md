# Nicholas Friends And Family Community

Send a message if you'd like an invite to this org.

#### Community Rules:

1. Respect Everyone
Treat all members with courtesy and respect. Harassment, hate speech, or any form of discrimination will not be tolerated.

3. No Spamming or Self-Promotion
Refrain from flooding the chat with repetitive messages or promoting personal projects excessively. Respect the community space.

5. Stay on Topic
Keep discussions relevant to the channel's theme or purpose. Off-topic conversations can be disruptive and should be taken to appropriate channels or private messages.

7. No NSFW Content
Any content of an explicit or mature nature is strictly prohibited. This includes text, images, links, and discussions.

9. Follow the Discord Community Guidelines
Follow Discord's Guidelines: Abide by Discord's rules at all times. Violations will result in action by moderators.

11. No Alternate Accounts
Do not use multiple accounts to bypass bans, evade restrictions, or deceive other members. Each member should have only one account in the server.

13. No Illegal Activities
Do not engage in or promote any illegal activities, including but not limited to piracy, hacking, or sharing illegal content.

15. No Impersonation
Do not impersonate other members, server staff, or individuals outside the server. This includes using similar usernames, profile pictures, or pretending to have authority you do not possess.

17. No Excessive Tagging
Avoid excessively tagging (@mentioning) other members, especially if it's not necessary. Respect others' notification settings and only tag when relevant.

19. Report Issues
If you encounter any violations of these rules or encounter any problems within the server, report them to moderators promptly.
