## Rules

**1. Respect Everyone**
<br>Treat all members with courtesy and respect. Harassment, hate speech, or any form of discrimination will not be tolerated.

**2. No Spamming or Self-Promotion**
<br>Refrain from flooding the chat with repetitive messages or promoting personal projects excessively. Respect the community space.

**3. Stay on Topic**
<br>Keep discussions relevant to the channel's theme or purpose. Off-topic conversations can be disruptive and should be taken to appropriate channels or private messages.

**4. No NSFW Content**
<br>Any content of an explicit or mature nature is strictly prohibited. This includes text, images, links, and discussions.

**5. Follow the Discord Community Guidelines**
<br>Abide by [Discord's Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines) at all times. Violations will result in action by moderators.

**6. No Alternate Accounts**
<br>Do not use multiple accounts to bypass bans, evade restrictions, or deceive other members. Each member should have only one account in the server.

**7. No Illegal Activities**
<br>Do not engage in or promote any illegal activities, including but not limited to piracy, hacking, or sharing illegal content.

**8. No Impersonation**
<br>Do not impersonate other members, server staff, or individuals outside the server. This includes using similar usernames, profile pictures, or pretending to have authority you do not possess.

**9. No Excessive Tagging**
<br>Avoid excessively tagging (@mentioning) other members, especially if it's not necessary. Respect others' notification settings and only tag when relevant.

**10. Report Issues**
<br>If you encounter any violations of these rules or encounter any problems within the server, report them to moderators promptly.

> By participating in this server, you agree to follow these rules. Thank you for helping keep our community a great place!
